What does this “Sniper V1” do and how does it work?

The bot allows you to buy any newly added token instantaniously (up to zero seconds from the time of the liquidity transaction show up in the mempool). It is called “frontrunning”. The bot works by connecting to the blockchain network, scanning for all mempool / pending transactions, finding and matching the liquidity add transaction of the token you are trying to snipe and then placing the buy order in the same block.

What is 'anti-tax', 'blacklist protection', 'mempool' and 'alphalytics' ?

Anti-tax: The most popular anti snipe measure, early buyers pay a penalty fee for using algorithms to get in early into tokens. The said penalty is often set as high as 90% leading to a total loss of funds for the user. We at Blockinetics stand strictly against such immoral practices and provide you with tools to circumvent the infamous sniper tax.

Blacklist protection: Another common anti snipe measure is the blacklist or the increased sell tax, even though technically different from one another, our blacklist sanity checks, guard you against both. A buyer which is added to the blacklist will not be able to sell the Token, and one that is caught in a sell tax will loose all his funds if he/she sells too early. Our siping algorithm reverts your transaction once it detects the danger to get caught up in it, and gets you into the token as soon as this immoral measure is lifted.

Mempool: We get it the Memory pool is a buzzword in crypto, often also referred to as the "dark forest" of Blockchains, the Memory Pool, Mempool or Txpool is a place where transactions are gathered before they are executed on the Blockchain. Here front running bots go on the hunt for incautious buyers to take advantage of too highly set slippage, and this is the place where our Sniper Bot identifies the Transaction that will open trading on a newly released coin. The Mempool is basically the Orderbook of a Dex and knowing what to look for you can achieve great speed gains when it comes to low latency trading. We are constantly adapting and speed optimizing our analytics to provide our users with the most competitive algorithm possible.

Alphalytics: This is an added to feature to the current alphalytics UI, you're provided with further dashboard focused on analytics, such as screening network usage, and chart indicators / general market maker insights.
