About us

Here at Blockinetics we send our engineers into the field to work directly with our customers, deploying our products, integrating their data, optimizing their workflows, and producing operational results in weeks. We help customers get the most out of our products and engineering expertise.

Our trading features are designed for individual use connected straight to your wallet where our systems do the rest for you. We help provide you with the latest trading bots specialising in sniper bots. Sniper bots (or “snipe bots”) are automated bots that monitor time-based activity and submit information at the very last moment, removing the opportunity for other people to respond to that action.

Sniper v1

SniperV1 is a low-latency Sniper bot that can access the memory pool particularly quickly, as it can rely on a speed-optimized fork of the Ethereum client Geth. The backend allows the user to conveniently and securely perform snipes on newly launched coins.
